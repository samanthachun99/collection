Canned food is great to use if you’re lazy or if you’re in the middle of a pandemic or apocalypse. Shelf life varies from a year to five, so you won’t have to worry about restocking very often. Everyone should have a variety of canned foods and donate some if able to. Some popular canned food choices are spam, corn, beans, and fruits like lychee.
