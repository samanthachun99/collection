Ritz is a circular cracker with ridge edges. Ritz are crunchy, but easy to bite into crackers, and are salted one side. Other varieties of Ritz exist, such as Ritz "Hint of Salt” and “Garlic Butter” flavor. They can be enjoyed on their own, with cheese, or with spreads and jams.
