Pocky is a snack food produced by Ezaki Glico. Pocky was first sold in 1966, and was invented by Yoshiaki Koma. They consist of chocolate-coated biscuit sticks. It was named after the Japanese onomatopoeic word pokkin. There are six different flavors all meant to be shared.
