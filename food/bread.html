Bread Loaves can be used for many things! Like toast, grilled cheese, ham and cheese sandwiches, or plain guilt eating. The first sliced loaf of bread was marketed in 1930 and is now a staple amongst many pantries. It's very popular in the grocery line up because of its versatility and great taste!

How to make “toast”
  1. Take a slice of bread
  2. Place in toaster
  3. Leave in until crispy
  4. Butter
  5. Optional: add jams, avocadoes, or additional toppings
